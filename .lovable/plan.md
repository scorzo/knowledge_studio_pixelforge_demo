

# PixelForge Printers Website

A clean, corporate printer company website inspired by Epson's design language — bold blue and white color scheme, professional layout, with skeleton screen placeholders throughout.

---

## Page 1: Homepage (SPA, Above-the-Fold)

- **Top navigation bar** with PixelForge logo on the left, nav links as skeleton bars (mimicking Store, Printers, Ink, etc.), a search bar skeleton, and a **"Sign In" button** (CTA linking to the login page)
- **Hero section** filling most of the viewport:
  - Left side: skeleton text blocks (headline + subheading area) with a skeleton CTA button
  - Right side: large skeleton image placeholder (where a product/person image would go)
- **Accent bar** — a bold blue stripe across the bottom of the hero (matching Epson's style)
- **Footer strip** with "PixelForge Inc." name, tagline "Precision in Every Pixel", and small skeleton links
- Color scheme: Epson-inspired — white background, deep blue (#003399) accents, light gray skeletons

## Page 2: Login Page

- Centered card layout on a clean white/light background
- PixelForge logo at the top of the card
- Skeleton input fields (email, password) with animated shimmer
- Skeleton "Sign In" button
- Skeleton "Forgot password?" and "Create account" link placeholders
- Subtle blue accent matching the homepage branding

## Design Details

- Skeleton screens use animated shimmer/pulse effect for a polished loading-state feel
- Fully responsive but optimized for desktop-first (matching the Epson reference)
- Blue (#003399) and white color palette with gray skeleton placeholders
- Clean, corporate typography feel
